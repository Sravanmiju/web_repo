Available Scholarships

Intern College supports students through various scholarships based on required qualifications.

  • Merit-Based Scholarship: For students with outstanding academic performance.
  • Need-Based Scholarship: For students from economically low backgrounds.
  • Sports Scholarship: For students with outstanding achievements in sports.
  • Minority & Special Category Scholarship: As per government .

To apply, download the Scholarship Application Form and submit it to the Office.
